His hammer and power stripped from him by Odin, Thor found himself in a small New Mexico town, where he proved he was worthy in a battle against the Destroyer.
Previous films have shown that Thor is not very skilled or comfortable as a brawler, nor does he have the raw fighting instinct as someone like the Hulk. This is reaffirmed again in Ragnarok, so clearly the Odinson hasn’t put much effort into shoring up this weakness so that it wouldn’t be as much of a sore spot. He’s definitely capable of holding his own when left unarmed, but he’s never shown winning a bout of fisticuffs with any force equal or greater to his own. Furthermore, the God of Thunder is 1500+ years old and has spent the vast majority of his fighting career with martial weapons and would thus need one relatively similar to what he was already familiar with in order to properly excel. Given that Infinity War is shown to happen some 20–30 minutes after the end of Ragnarok, he didn’t exactly have time to train himself in the proper use of the Odinforce, and it’s very likely besides that he still lacks proper discipline to use it on command. And against someone like Thanos, who thrashed Hulk in under two minutes WITHOUT using an Infinity Stone in full view of Thor, Odinson knows that “good enough” will never cut it in the fight to come, and he can’t afford to try for it.
